    RepairOS

    Professional Automotive Repair Management Platform

    RepairOS is a comprehensive management platform designed for automotive repair shops, parts sellers, and multi-location businesses. It streamlines operations from customer intake and repair order management to billing, inventory tracking, and team coordination.

    Key Features

    Repair Order Management

    Create, track, and manage repair orders from intake to completion with real-time status updates.

    Customer Management

    Maintain detailed customer profiles, vehicle history, and communication logs.

    Billing & Invoicing

    Generate invoices, track payments, manage overdue accounts, and process transactions.

    Parts & Inventory

    Track parts stock levels, manage suppliers, and automate reorder notifications.

    Multi-Tenant Support

    Manage multiple locations or organizations with isolated data and shared user accounts.

    Reports & Analytics

    Generate detailed reports on revenue, technician performance, and business metrics.
